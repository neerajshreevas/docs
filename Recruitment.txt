# Conversive Recruitment Journey Workflow (Mintlify Prototype)

Based on recruitment workflow best practices and standard ATS onboarding flow—from requisition to onboarding—the ideal recruitment journey includes Job Requisition → Candidate Pipeline → Interview Management → Offer → Onboarding. Typical stages include New Applications → Screening → Phone Screen → Interview → Assessment → Reference Check → Offer → Hired → Onboarding ([Mintlify][1])

---

## Workflow: Conversive Recruitment Journey

```text
START
   ↓
1. Hiring Request Raised
   ↓
2. Job Requisition Approval
   ↓
3. Job Posting Published
   ↓
4. Candidate Application Submission
   ↓
5. Resume Screening
   ↓
6. HR Shortlisting
   ↓
7. Initial HR Interview
   ↓
8. Technical / Functional Interview
   ↓
9. Leadership / Final Interview
   ↓
10. Candidate Evaluation & Feedback
   ↓
11. Offer Approval
   ↓
12. Offer Letter Release
   ↓
13. Candidate Acceptance
   ↓
14. Pre-boarding Documentation
   ↓
15. IT + Admin + HR Onboarding
   ↓
16. Day-1 Joining Experience
   ↓
17. Probation + Confirmation
   ↓
END
```

# Detailed Step-by-Step Workflow for Mintlify

---

## Phase 1 — Requirement Identification

### Step 1: Hiring Request Raised

### Owner:

Hiring Manager

### Actions:

* Raise manpower request
* Define role
* Department details
* Budget approval
* Justification for hiring

### Output:

Approved Hiring Request

---

## Phase 2 — Recruitment Initiation

### Step 2: Job Requisition Approval

### Owner:

HR + Finance + Leadership

### Actions:

* Validate business need
* Budget verification
* Final approval

### Output:

Approved Job Requisition

---

## Phase 3 — Candidate Attraction

### Step 3: Job Posting Published

### Channels:

* Company Career Page
* LinkedIn
* Naukri
* Referrals
* Internal Job Board

### Output:

Applications Received

---

## Phase 4 — Candidate Screening

### Step 4: Resume Screening

### Owner:

Talent Acquisition Team

### Actions:

* Resume review
* Qualification match
* Experience validation
* Skill relevance check

### Output:

Shortlisted Candidates

---

## Phase 5 — Interview Process

### Step 5: Multi-Level Interviews

### Stages:

### Round 1:

HR Screening

### Round 2:

Technical Assessment

### Round 3:

Hiring Manager Discussion

### Round 4:

Leadership / Cultural Fit

### Output:

Final Selection

Structured interview scheduling and scorecards improve consistency and candidate experience ([Mintlify][1])

---

## Phase 6 — Selection & Offer

### Step 6: Offer Management

### Actions:

* Salary fitment
* Approval workflow
* Offer release
* Negotiation handling

### Output:

Offer Accepted

---

## Phase 7 — Preboarding

### Step 7: Documentation Collection

### Documents:

* PAN / Aadhaar
* Educational Certificates
* Previous Employment Proof
* Bank Details
* Address Proof
* NDA / Compliance Forms

### Output:

Ready for Joining

Digital document collection reduces delays and improves onboarding efficiency ([recruitonline.ai][2])

---

## Phase 8 — Onboarding

### Step 8: Joining Preparation

### Teams Involved:

* HR
* IT
* Admin
* Reporting Manager

### Actions:

* Laptop allocation
* ID card
* Email access
* System access
* Welcome kit
* Induction schedule

### Output:

Successful Day-1 Joining

---

## Phase 9 — Employee Integration

### Step 9: Confirmation Journey

### Actions:

* 30-60-90 day review
* Training completion
* Performance review
* Confirmation letter

### Output:

Employee Confirmed
