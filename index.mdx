# Recruitment Journey

Conversive’s Recruitment Journey is designed to create a seamless, structured, and high-conversion hiring experience—from manpower planning to successful employee onboarding.

A strong recruitment workflow improves hiring speed, candidate experience, and internal accountability by clearly defining each stage, ownership, approvals, and SLAs. Modern onboarding best practices include pre-boarding, Day 1 readiness, training, payroll setup, and role-based integration to accelerate productivity. :contentReference\[oaicite:0\]{index=0}
